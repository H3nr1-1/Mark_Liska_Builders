Roofing / Siding

Roofing services include installing new roofs, replacing old or damaged roofing materials, repairing leaks, and performing regular maintenance. Common materials used are asphalt shingles, metal, tile, and flat roofing systems.

Siding services involve installing and repairing the exterior walls of buildings using materials like vinyl, wood, fiber cement, or aluminum. These services improve both the durability and aesthetic of a structure, while also offering insulation and weather protection.

Renovations

Interior renovations: remodeling kitchens, bathrooms, basements, or entire living spaces. Services often involve flooring, drywall, painting, electrical, and plumbing upgrades.

Exterior renovations: upgrading siding, windows, doors, decks, and landscaping to improve curb appeal and energy efficiency.

Flooring

Installation includes laying down materials such as hardwood, laminate, vinyl, or tile in residential or commercial spaces.

Repairs and refinishing address issues like scratches, water damage, or wear and tear, especially for wood and tile floors.

Replacement services remove old flooring and install new materials to update the look and feel of a space.

Tile Work

Installation includes placing ceramic, porcelain, stone, or glass tiles in areas like kitchens, bathrooms, floors, walls, and backsplashes.

Repair and replacement services address cracked, chipped, or loose tiles, as well as regrouting and resealing to restore appearance and prevent water damage.

Custom tile work offers decorative patterns, mosaics, and tailored designs to enhance the style and value of a space.
